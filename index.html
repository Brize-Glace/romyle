About Me

Hi, I'm Romyle, real name Romain Viktorovitch, born in German-speaking Switzerland in Winterthur near Zurich. I'm 17 years old and currently living in Paris, France. I'm a beginner EDM-House composer. I've been passionate about music since my early years. Nowadays, I particularly enjoy listening to EDM. Artists that inspire me include ILLENIUM, Said The Sky, Rival, Blanke, LUVIUM, Culture Code, Annkia Wells, and various others. These artists have fueled my desire to start creating EDM music.
